True Princess

Even though Edward was very pleased to have been promoted to Prince, all he still really wanted to do was take a nap. So the queen laid her hand upon Edward, and he happily lay down in the grass for a long well deserved rest, while Noel danced around the queen in the beautiful sunset, a True Princess at last.
